## Coverage in this ingestion

The source shows the statute as current as at 19 Apr 2026 and lists a 12-part structure including offences, tribunals, and collective management regulation. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The captured body includes preliminary provisions (sections 1-6) and reciprocal/non-reciprocal country mechanisms. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

## Key captured points

The long title indicates the Act repealed and re-enacted earlier copyright legislation and covers copyright plus protection of performances and related rights. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Section 1 captures staged commencement for specific Part 9 and section 500-series provisions via ministerial notification. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Sections 3 and 4 provide a framework for extending or limiting operation in relation to foreign countries based on reciprocity and adequacy of protection. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Act binds Government, but the Government is not liable to be prosecuted for offences under this Act. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

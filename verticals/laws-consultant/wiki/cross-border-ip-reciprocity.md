# Cross Border IP Reciprocity

**Summary**: Concept page on reciprocating and non-reciprocating country mechanisms from the captured preliminary sections of Singapore's Copyright Act 2021.  

**Sources**:
- Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-19

---

The captured sections 3 and 4 provide for regulations designating countries as reciprocating or non-reciprocating for selected applications of the Act. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Reciprocating status in the captured text depends on treaty alignment or adequate protection under foreign law, for both works and performances. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Non-reciprocating designations in the captured text may limit or modify application where foreign protection is considered inadequate. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The captured language also states that regulations must not remove rights acquired before publication of the regulations. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

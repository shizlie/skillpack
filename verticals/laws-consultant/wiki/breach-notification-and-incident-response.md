# Breach Notification And Incident Response

**Summary**: Cross-instrument incident handling page linking PDPA and Cybersecurity Act structures with MAS TRM operational incident response expectations for financial institutions.

---

The PDPA table of contents in the captured source includes a dedicated Part 6A on notification of data breaches. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Cybersecurity Act captured text defines "cybersecurity incident" and includes a dedicated Part 4 on responses to cybersecurity threats and incidents. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The MAS TRM guideline expects an incident management framework to restore affected IT services/systems to secure and stable states quickly, with clear process, escalation and decision responsibilities. (source: Technology Risk Management Guidelines - Monetary Authority of Singapore.md)

## Working note

The MAS TRM guideline also expects supporting evidence preservation, continuous event/alert monitoring for early detection, and crisis communication planning that includes customer and media communication procedures. (source: Technology Risk Management Guidelines - Monetary Authority of Singapore.md)

# Cybersecurity Service Regulation

**Summary**: Concept page on licensable cybersecurity services based on the captured definitional sections of the Cybersecurity Act 2018.  

**Sources**:
- Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-19

---

The Act's table of contents includes Part 5 "Cybersecurity Service Providers" and a Second Schedule on "Licensable cybersecurity services." (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The interpretation section captures a broad definition of "cybersecurity service," including testing, forensic examination, incident response, monitoring, managed controls, advice, and training when provided for reward. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The captured text also lists explicit exclusions (for example, certain software sales and general network management aimed at availability/performance). (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Licence administration mechanics and compliance duties are not fully captured in the current raw excerpt and remain to be verified. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

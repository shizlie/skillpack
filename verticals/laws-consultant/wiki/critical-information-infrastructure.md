# Critical Information Infrastructure

**Summary**: Definition and scope anchor for CII under the captured Cybersecurity Act text.  

**Sources**:
- Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-19

---

The captured interpretation provision defines "critical information infrastructure" as a computer or computer system with a designation in force under section 7(1). (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The application clause captured indicates that Part 3 (except section 8) applies to CII located wholly or partly in Singapore. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The First Schedule is identified in the table of contents as listing essential services relevant to CII framing. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Detailed owner duties and enforcement pathways are not present in the clipped text and need a full statute export for complete compliance interpretation. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

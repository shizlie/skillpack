## Coverage by instrument (from current captures)

The Computer Misuse Act capture focuses on unauthorised access/modification concepts and foundational definitions tied to computer misuse and scam-related references. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Cybersecurity Act capture frames state administration, CII regulation, threat/incident response, and cybersecurity service provider regulation. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The PDPA capture frames organisational handling of personal data, including a table-of-contents reference to breach notification and enforcement pathways. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Copyright Act capture frames rights/performance protection with cross-border reciprocity mechanisms and staged commencement for selected provisions. (source: Copyright Act 2021 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The MAS TRM guideline (January 2021) adds supervisory best-practice controls for FIs across governance, secure development, IT operations, resilience, cyber incident response, cyber testing, and online financial services security. (source: Technology Risk Management Guidelines - Monetary Authority of Singapore.md)

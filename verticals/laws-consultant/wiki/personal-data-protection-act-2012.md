## Key captured points

The purpose clause balances individual protection interests and organisations' needs to collect/use/disclose data for reasonable purposes. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

"Personal data" is defined as data about an identifiable individual, whether directly or via other accessible information. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The application section states that core parts do not impose obligations on individuals acting domestically, employees acting in course of employment, and public agencies, among others. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Data intermediary carve-outs are captured, while preserving that the principal organisation remains responsible for personal data processed on its behalf. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The table of contents explicitly includes Part 6A on notification of data breaches and Part 9B on offences affecting personal data and anonymised information. (source: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

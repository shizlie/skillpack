## Coverage in this ingestion

The source shows the statute status as current as at 19 Apr 2026. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The table of contents in the capture includes Part 2 "Offences" and Part 3 "Miscellaneous and General", but the body text captured here mostly includes Part 1 and interpretation language. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The long title states that the Act addresses unauthorised access or modification of computer material and abuse of the national digital identity service. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

## Key interpreted terms captured

The definition of "computer" is broad and includes interconnected or related devices with processing/storage functions, while excluding listed non-programmable devices. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

"Damage" is defined with multiple thresholds/effects, including monetary loss and impacts to medical care, safety, or public health/public safety. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Act explicitly defines when access is "unauthorised" and when modification is "unauthorised," using entitlement-plus-consent tests. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

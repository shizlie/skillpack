## Coverage in this ingestion

The source capture includes enactment metadata, commencement dates for Parts 1-4 and 6 (31 Aug 2018), and commencement of Part 5 and the Second Schedule (11 Apr 2022). (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The captured table of contents includes administration, CII regulation, responses to threats/incidents, cybersecurity service providers, and general provisions. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

## Key captured points

The long title frames three major controls: threat/incident response powers, regulation of CII owners, and regulation of cybersecurity service providers. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The interpretation provision defines critical information infrastructure as a computer/system with designation in force under section 7(1). (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

"Cybersecurity service" is defined broadly and includes testing, incident response, managed monitoring/control activities, advisory, and training activities performed for reward. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

Application language captured states that Part 3 (except section 8) applies to CII located wholly or partly in Singapore, and section 8 applies to any computer/system located wholly or partly in Singapore. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

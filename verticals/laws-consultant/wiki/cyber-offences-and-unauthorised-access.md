# Cyber Offences And Unauthorised Access

**Summary**: Concept page for unauthorised access/modification framing from the captured Computer Misuse Act text and its relationship to broader cybersecurity governance law.

## Core framing from current captures

The Computer Misuse Act defines what constitutes securing access to programs/data and when such access is unauthorised. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The same Act also defines when modifications to computer contents are unauthorised, using entitlement and consent concepts. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

The Cybersecurity Act separately defines "cybersecurity incident" and "cybersecurity threat" in ways focused on system security state and impacts. (source: Cybersecurity Act 2018 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)

## Practical linkage

In this source set, the Computer Misuse Act reads as offence-oriented groundwork while the Cybersecurity Act reads as governance/administrative framework, especially for CII and service providers. (source: Computer Misuse Act 1993 - Singapore Statutes Online.md)
